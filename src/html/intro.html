CILT — Collaborative Image Labeling Tool

CILT is an end-to-end platform that overlaps data labeling and training toolset with digital collaborative space. With it, you can build an entire ML pipeline from square one. Manage all your data operations in one place and accomplish the ultimate quality of your ML models through CILT’s intuitive interface and scalable tooling. Stable hosting and secure cloud storage to cache your data included. Created by hands-on ML practitioners.
